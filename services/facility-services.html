Turnkey Facility Services


DVM’s capabilities in Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing (MEP) are built around providing feasible and economically viable designs. We have a dedicated maintenance division with several trucks on the road, servicing a wide variety of industries and helping in assist our clients in developing the proper maintenance programs to ensure long life, efficiency and lower their failure rates. Our in-house mechanical and electrical engineering teams allow us to, efficiently and economically, complete any job of any size and complexity.

We offer installation, service and maintenance, engineering and troubleshooting services for Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing. With our energy and sustainability background we work with our clients to help design the most efficient operating system for their facility. We understand that it is not a cookie cutter business and each solution is tailored around our client’s specific needs.
