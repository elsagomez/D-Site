Water Conservation Systems

Designing Building Conservation Systems with up to 60% Savings on Water

DVM develops, designs, engineers and installs water conservation and desinfection systems for your property. The technology we implement is an innovative water conservation system that focuses on meeting a facilities goals in reducing gallons per minute being used.

The savings have been recorded up to 23.5% of the overall water usage. Our systems are legally recognized by the local governing bodies and installed compliance with local and federal codes accompanied with permits and engineered drawings.

DVM Water Systems

DVM Water Systems are custom design for each application to address the needs for each unique facility. RThis system will reduce the gallons per minute used throughout your entire water system. The system is automated and intelligent with built in fail safes to guaranty 100% up-time, and continuous monitoring to ensure water safety levels, possible leaks and savings.

DVM Water Systems GPM 2K series is an innovative water conservation system that focuses on meeting a facilities goals in reducing gallons per minute being used. The savings have been recorded up to 23.5% of the overall water usage. The GPM 2K series is designed as an intelligent water conservation system that adjusts to changes in the current water pressure and reducing any unnecessary amounts of water into the facility at any given time.

Furthermore, the system will alert you of any possible water leaks, monthly savings and added options to control remote shut offs in case of excessive water leaks or flooding.
