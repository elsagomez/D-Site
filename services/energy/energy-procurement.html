Energy Procurement

Offset the capital cost of your energy projects

DVMs Energy Procurement expertise and services can enable your company to obtain the best rates and contract terms for electricity and natural gas. Our specialists provide energy market analysis and reporting, energy planning, forecasting and negotiating with suppliers both current and future.

With so many energy suppliers available in the market, it is hard to find which company offers the best rates and benefits for a business. Considering that, DVM holds reverse auctions with the most competitive energy suppliers exclusively for your company. Through a real-time platform, all suppliers are invited to bid and counter-bid until the lowest rates in the market are uncovered. This way, we can assure our clients the best rates in the market in a fair, quick and transparent competition.

Our Approach

DVM creates competition among multiple ESCOs for both: pricing and terms, and we leverage our relationships and aggreagate purcasing power to deliver unmatched results.

Using a propietary platform called Strategic Relationship Pricing (SRP), our system personally engages these companies to create competition and negotiate rates and terms on your behalf, ensuring that our clients receive the best price at the best terms. No Surprise Fees or Tariffs.

DVM is considered a "Power Advisor" because of our voluminous portfolio with every one of these companies, and they aggressively compete daily for our business.

DVM also provides quarterly analysis reports so clients know how their energy purchases are performing compared to the utility markets
