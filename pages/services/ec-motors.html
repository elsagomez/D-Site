DVM Motors Solutions

The Strategic Horsepower to Drive Efficiency.



EC motors (Electronically Commutated Motor) have electronic commutation systems, rather than mechanical commutators and brushes. An EC Motor has an electronic control with internal microprocessor and memory that allows for variability capabilities control, and it is typically 30% to 70% more efficienct than standard motors.

  • Permanent magnet rotor allows for less losses that leads to better efficiency
  • Electronic control with internal microprocessor and memory allow for variability and control capabilities or “controllability”
  • Similar to an integral horsepower motor with an external VFD
  • Typically 20-50% more efficient than standard efficiency motors

What is Electronically Commutated Motors (ECM) Technology?
  • A. Communication from Air Handler demand (heat, cool, fan...)
  • B. The Microprocessor constantly monitors motor speed (RPM) or torque (current)
  • C. The Microprocessor continously directs the cntrols to adjust current (torque) and /or frequency (speed) to maintain designed program


Benefits

  • Recondition equipment vs replace
  • 30 – 70% More Efficient – High Energy Savings
  • Payback 1-3 Years
  • Precise and Controlled Operation
  • Wider Range of Applications
  • Reduced noise under speed control
  • Incentive and financial opportunities available
  • Reduced maintenance

Applications

  • Fan powered termimnal box
  • Fan coil units
  • Rooftop condensers
  • Rooftop Package Units
  • Large Air Handler
  • Fan Filter Units (FFU)
  • Commercial Refrigeration
  • Pool Motors
