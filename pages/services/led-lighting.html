LED Lighting Solutions

Innovative LED Lighting Solutions and Lighting Management.

DVM Energy provides turnkey LED lighting services including the audit/survey, controls configuration, installation, engineering and design, project management and rebate management. With our team of experts, we evaluate the facility at no cost to the client and provide recommendations to optimize savings while maintaining or improving light levels. We provide a full ROI analysis to the client including any available rebates and a 10 year cash flow analysis.

Additionally, we have an in-house team of electricians that allows clients to reduce installation costs, while providing better control over the project

DVM offers high level consulting and turnkey lighting solutions that include: Feasibility Analysis, Financial Analysis, Product Sourcing and Installation, Rebate and Grants Management and Post Care Services.

Step 1: Consultation

DVM’s in-house team of electricians and engineers can recommend technical solutions and discuss the benefits of different lighting technologies to help you visualize, not only the econmical benefits, but also the comfort benefits that your tenants will receive by upgrading to an LED system.

Step 2: Value Engineering

Our value engineering approach allows us to cross-match ou client’s lighting specifications with alternative fixtures that meet their budget, design goals and schedules. The better we understand your project needs, the better we can select better alternative lighting that retain styling, improve efficiency and adjust to your budget.

Step 3: Supply and Installation

DVM will order, deliver and install your new lighting system without disrupting your daily operations and dispose of the old fixtures. We will also arrange all pre and post-inspections necessary to secure any grants or rebates available.

Step 4: Financial Assesement

DVM will provide a full Return on Investment (ROI) analysis report to the client, including any available rebate, incentive or grant and a 10 year cash flow analysis. We will handle the paperwork and process of your financial incentive.
